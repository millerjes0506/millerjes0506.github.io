About Me

My journey to where I am today has been quite an adventure. I currently live and work in Mexico, Missouri but originally from Charleston, Illinois. I have taught for over ten years, landing my first teaching job in January 2012. I have taught high school and middle school math and expanded my experience in teaching science and robotics. I have four wonderful boys; Jayden (13), Grayson (7), Jaxson (4), and Wesley (2). I met my husband, Chad, during my student teaching in 2011, and we married in 2015. My undergraduate and first master's degrees were earned at Eastern Illinois University in Mathematics. I am currently earning a learning specialist degree in Instructional Design and plan to be completed with this program in 2024. Once I complete my degree in Instructional Design, I plan to move out of the classroom to find a job somewhere using my newfound degree.
