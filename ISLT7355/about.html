About Me

A Little About Me

Hello everyone. My name is Jessica Miller. I live and work in Mexico, Missouri. I teach middle school math, science, and robotics at the Missouri Military Academy. I have four wonderful boys, Jayden (13), Grayson (7), Jaxson (4), and Wesley (2).
This is my second semester at Mizzou. I do not have much experience with Web Design. I think I did a little web design when I was in high school. I honestly can’t wait to really dive into this course and have so much fun. I am not sure what I want to do with my newly found knowledge, but I’m excited to see what all I learn. Once I complete my degree in Instructional Design, I plan to move out of the classroom. Every summer I start running. Running a half marathon is on my bucket list! One hobby that I enjoy but wish I had more time for is sewing.
